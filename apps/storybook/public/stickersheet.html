Kilo Cloud — visual reference

A printable overview of every token, surface, and component declared in DESIGN.md. Dark-first, near-black, utilitarian. Borders carry the elevation. Yellow acts — it's the brand and the CTA at once. Greys do everything else.

Do's & Don'ts

The rules that keep Kilo Cloud feeling like Kilo Cloud and not a generic dark dashboard.
Do
Use brand yellow for the primary action — once per surface. Brand and CTA are the same swatch.
Don't
Don't put two yellows on a screen. The second action is always a secondary.
Do
Borders are white at 10%. Lift comes from value step + translucent border.
surface · 10% white border
Don't
Don't use solid grey borders. They read flat against every surface step.
solid #525252 — wrong
Do
Pair primary yellow with secondary charcoal or ghost-link cancels.
Don't
Don't use blue as a button background. Blue is a legacy inline-link role only.
Do
Sentence case for every user-visible label. Buttons, nav, titles.
"Create session" · "Buy credits" · "Invite member"
Don't
Don't Title Case. Don't Use Exclamation Points!
"Create Session" · "Buy Credits!" — wrong
Do
Dollars, latencies, timestamps in Roboto Mono. They align across rows.

Don't
Don't use Inter for table numerics. Variable widths jitter the column.
